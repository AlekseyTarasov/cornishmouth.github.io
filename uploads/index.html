Innovative negative ionizing technology that works in simple way.

Millions of negative ions attach to positive charged particles such as germs, pollen, dust and other allergens and air pollutants causing them to become too heavy to stay airborne. At this point they drop to floor or attach to nearby surfaces.

So, negative ions remove them from the air you breathe and prevent them from causing respiratory problems and other health issues.
